Steak

Air fried steak that will make you drool!!

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Air Fryer
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Patience

Steps

  1. Leave Steaks for 30 min in Room Tem4p
  2. Season with Salt + Pepper
  3. Set air fryer for 400c
  4. Cook steaks for 15min and flip halfway
  5. Enjoy!
